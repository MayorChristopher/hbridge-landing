Our Mission

Nigeria's health data
deserves a digital home.

Most Nigerians carry their medical history in paper folders scattered between clinics, hospitals, and pharmacies. Records are lost. Diagnoses are repeated. Time and money are wasted.

Hbridge is an Electronic Medical Records (EMR) platform built specifically for Nigeria. Every patient gets a secure, lifelong digital health vault. Every doctor gets instant access to the records they need to make better decisions, faster.

End-to-end secure

PIN-locked vault, encrypted at rest, full access control.

Patient + Doctor

Patients own their data. Doctors receive it instantly.

How the EMR Works

Organised in
under 3 minutes

From signup to a fully organised medical records vault — completely on your phone.

1

Create your secure vault

Sign up and set a private PIN. No one — not even Hbridge — can access your records without your PIN.

2

Upload and organise

Create folders per hospital or doctor. Upload lab results, prescriptions, imaging, and clinical notes — all in one place.

3

Share with any doctor

When you see a doctor, share your exact records with one tap. You control what they see and for how long — revoke access any time.
