Our Mission

Bridging the gap between
patients and care

Hbridge was founded on a simple belief: quality healthcare should not be a privilege. In Nigeria, millions of people lack access to the doctors and specialists they need — not because those doctors don't exist, but because there is no reliable way to find, consult, and follow up with them.

We built Hbridge to change that. Our platform removes every barrier between a patient and their doctor — from appointment scheduling and real-time consultations, to secure medical record management and emergency response — putting the full power of modern healthcare in every Nigerian's pocket.

Built for Nigeria

Designed around the healthcare realities of African patients and providers.

Verified & Trusted

Every doctor is credentialed and verified before joining the platform.

Features

Everything healthcare,
one app

Hbridge brings together every layer of healthcare access, designed to work seamlessly for patients and healthcare providers alike.

Real-time Consultations

Chat directly with a verified doctor in seconds. Send photos, voice notes, documents, and get professional medical advice without leaving home.

Appointment Booking

Browse doctor schedules and book appointments in real time. Get reminders, manage your bookings, and never miss a consultation.

Medical Records Vault

Store your medical history, lab results, and prescriptions in a secure PIN-protected vault. Share records with your doctor with one tap.

Emergency Services

One-tap SOS alert with location sharing. Connect to emergency responders and notify your emergency contacts instantly when it matters most.

Doctor Profiles

Browse verified doctors by specialty, location, and availability. Read reviews, check credentials, and choose the right doctor for your needs.

AI Health Assistant

Get instant answers to health questions, symptom checks, and medication guidance — powered by AI, always available, and privacy-first.
