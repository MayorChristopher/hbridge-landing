Our Mission

Nigeria's health data
deserves a digital home

Today, most Nigerians carry their medical history in paper folders, scattered between clinics, hospitals, and pharmacies. Records are lost. Diagnoses are repeated. Time and money are wasted.

Hbridge is an Electronic Medical Records (EMR) platform built specifically for Nigeria. It gives every patient a secure, lifelong digital health vault — and gives doctors instant access to the records they need to make better decisions, faster.

End-to-end secure

PIN-protected vault, encrypted storage, and controlled sharing.

Doctor + Patient

Designed for both sides — patients own their data, doctors get it instantly.

Electronic Medical Records Vault

Your lifelong, PIN-secured medical records vault. Store lab results, prescriptions, diagnoses, imaging, and clinical notes — organised by doctor or hospital. Share any record with a verified doctor in one tap, with full access control and audit trail.

How The EMR Works

Your records, organised
in under 3 minutes

From signup to a fully organised medical record vault — completely on your phone.

Step 1

Create your secure vault

Sign up and set a private PIN to protect your medical vault. No one else — not even Hbridge — can access your records.

Step 2

Upload & organise your records

Create folders for each hospital and doctor you visit. Upload PDFs, images of prescriptions, lab results, X-rays — all in one place.

Step 3

Share with any doctor, instantly

When you see a doctor, share your exact records with one tap. No printing, no carrying, no explaining from memory. You control what they can see and for how long.
